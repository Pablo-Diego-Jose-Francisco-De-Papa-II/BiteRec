How the model works 🤖

A Random-Forest classifier is trained on seven nutrients (energy, sugars, fat, saturated fat, protein, salt, fibre) to predict a product's Nutri-Score grade. We then read the model two ways: its class probabilities give a smooth 0–100 health score for ranking, and SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) reveals exactly how much each nutrient pushed a given prediction — the same approach used in our off_nutriscore_01.ipynb notebook.
